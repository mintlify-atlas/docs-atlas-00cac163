Trackmart offers flexible authentication options to make it easy for you to get started. You can sign up and log in using either your phone number or email address.

## Sign Up for a New Account

Creating your Trackmart account is quick and simple. Follow these steps to get started:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Trackmart app">
    Launch the app on your mobile device. You'll see the welcome screen with the Trackmart logo and tagline "Order • Track • Build".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose your authentication method">
    You can sign up using either:
    - **Phone number** (default)
    - **Email address** (tap "Use Email instead" to switch)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Depending on your chosen method:
    
    **For Phone Authentication:**
    - Select your country code (defaults to Kenya +254)
    - Enter your phone number
    - Create a secure password
    - Enter your name
    
    **For Email Authentication:**
    - Enter your email address
    - Create a secure password
    - Enter your name
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Agree to Terms of Use">
    Check the box to agree to Trackmart's Terms of Use. You can tap on "Terms of use" to review them.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete verification">
    **Phone:** You'll receive an SMS code. Enter the code when prompted to verify your phone number.
    
    **Email:** Check your inbox for a verification link. Click the link to verify your email address, then return to the app to log in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Phone verification is typically faster, as the SMS code is often retrieved automatically.
</Note>

## Logging In to Your Account

Returning users can quickly access their accounts:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Launch Trackmart on your device.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Ensure you're on the login screen">
    If you see "New to Trackmart? Sign up", you're on the login screen. Otherwise, tap "Log in".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    **Phone Login:**
    - Select your country code
    - Enter your phone number
    - Enter your password
    
    **Email Login:**
    - Tap "Use Email instead"
    - Enter your email address
    - Enter your password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap Login">
    The app will verify your credentials and sign you in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Password Recovery

Forgot your password? No problem:

<Accordion title="Reset Password for Email Accounts">
  1. On the login screen, tap "Use Email instead"
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Tap "Forgot password?"
  4. Check your email inbox for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password
  6. Return to the app and log in with your new password
</Accordion>

<Warning>
  Password reset is currently only available for email-based accounts. If you use phone authentication and forget your password, please contact Trackmart support.
</Warning>

## Switching Authentication Methods

You can easily switch between phone and email authentication:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Phone to Email" icon="envelope">
    Tap "Use Email instead" on the login or sign-up screen to switch to email authentication.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Email to Phone" icon="phone">
    Tap "Use Phone instead" on the login or sign-up screen to switch to phone authentication.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I didn't receive my SMS verification code">
    - Check that you entered the correct phone number
    - Wait a few minutes, as SMS delivery can be delayed
    - Check your SMS/message inbox for any filtered messages
    - Try using email authentication instead
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="My email verification link isn't working">
    - Check that you clicked the correct link from the verification email
    - Ensure the link hasn't expired (verify within 24 hours)
    - Try requesting a new verification email by signing up again
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I can't log in with my credentials">
    - Double-check that you're using the correct authentication method (phone vs. email)
    - Verify that your password is correct (passwords are case-sensitive)
    - If using email, ensure your email is verified
    - Try resetting your password if you use email authentication
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
