Trackmart provides multiple ways to get help and support when you need it. Access helpful resources, contact our team, or learn more about the app.

## Accessing Help

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the navigation menu">
    From the home screen, tap the menu icon (three horizontal lines) in the top-left corner to open the navigation drawer.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to help section">
    Scroll through the navigation drawer to find:
    - **Help** - Access FAQs and common questions
    - **Contact Us** - Get in touch with support
    - **About** - Learn about Trackmart
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select your option">
    Tap on the appropriate menu item based on what you need.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Support Workflow

Here's the recommended approach when you need help:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the Help page first">
    Start with the FAQs - most common questions are answered here. This gives you immediate answers without waiting.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review relevant guides">
    Check the specific feature guides in the documentation for detailed instructions on ordering, tracking, payments, or messaging.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Contact support if needed">
    If you still need assistance, use the Contact page to reach out via phone, email, or WhatsApp.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide details">
    When contacting support, include:
    - What you were trying to do
    - What went wrong or what you're confused about
    - Any error messages you saw
    - Your device type (Android/iOS)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting Before Contacting Support

Try these steps before reaching out:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="App not loading or crashing">
    - Close and restart the app
    - Check your internet connection
    - Clear app cache (Settings → Apps → Trackmart → Clear Cache)
    - Update to the latest app version
    - Restart your device
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't log in">
    - Verify you're using the correct phone number or email
    - Check that you have internet connectivity
    - For phone auth, ensure you're receiving SMS messages
    - For email auth, check your spam folder for verification emails
    - Try the password reset option if using email
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Orders not showing">
    - Pull down to refresh the order list
    - Check that you're connected to the internet
    - Verify you're logged into the correct account
    - Check the order status filter/tab
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Map not loading">
    - Enable location permissions in device settings
    - Check internet connection
    - Verify GPS is enabled on your device
    - Try switching between WiFi and mobile data
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
Never share your account password or verification codes with anyone, including support staff. Legitimate support will never ask for these details.
</Warning>
